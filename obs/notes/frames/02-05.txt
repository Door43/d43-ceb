===== Disobedience [02-05] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?nolink&}}

The woman saw that the fruit was beautiful and looked delicious. She also wanted to be **wise**, so she picked some of the fruit and ate it. Then she gave some to her husband, **who was with her**, and he ate it, too.


===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[ceb:obe:kt:wise]]**

==== Translation Notes: ====

  * **maalamon**- Ang babaye gusto nga makabaton ug panghunahuna ug pagsabot nga sama sa bitin, ug sama sa naangkon sa Dios.
  * **nga kauban niya**- Kini mahinungdanong masayran tungod kay kini nagpakita nga ang lalaki anaa sa dihang ang babaye nagdesisyon sa pagkaon sa bunga.
