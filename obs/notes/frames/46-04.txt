===== God Sent Ananias to Saul [46-04] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-04.jpg?nolink}}

There was a **disciple** in Damascus named Ananias. **God** said to him, “Go to the house where **Saul** is staying. Place your hands on him so that he can see again.” **But Ananias said**, “**Master**, I have heard how this man has **persecuted** the believers.” **God answered him**, “Go! I have chosen him **to declare my name** to the Jews and to people from other people groups. He will **suffer** many things **for my name**.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Apan niingon si Ananias:**  Kung dili klaro nganong gigamit ang pulong nga “Apan,” hubara usab kini sama sa, “Apan nahadlok si Ananias ug miingon.”
  * **Nitubag ang Dios:**  Mahubad kini nga, "Aron isiguro gayod si Ananias, busa miingon ang Dios kaniya”  aron masabtan pa nganong gisulti kini sa Dios.
  * **ipaila ang akong ngalan:**  Nagpasabot kini nga, "magtudlo siya bahin kanako” o, “aron mahimo ako nga inila.”
  * **alang sa akong ngalan:**  Nagpasabot kini nga, “alang kanako” o, “tungod kanako” o, “tungod kay gialagaran ko niya” o, “tungod kay gitudloan niya ang uban bahin kanako.”
