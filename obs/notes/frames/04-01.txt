<h2>Translation Notes:</h2>

<div class="level2">\\
<ul>\\
<li class="level1"><b>many years after the flood</b> - Many generations had passed since the end of the flood.</li>\\
<li class="level1"><b>again many people</b> - Noah's family multiplied into enough people to fill a city.</li>\\
<li class="level1"><b>same language</b> - This means there was only one language, so they could all understand each other.</li>\\
<li class="level1"><b>a city</b> - It is best to use a general word for "city" since the text does not give a specific name.</li>\\
</ul>
