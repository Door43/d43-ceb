====== Open Bible Stories (ceb) ======

**50 ka mahinungdanong mga istorya gikan sa Biblia, gikan sa Pagpamuhat sa Dios hangtod sa Pinadayag, aron gamiton sa pagsangyaw ug padisipulo ug pagpanudlo, ug gisulat kini, gihimong audio, video, nga mabutang sa silpon (mobile phone), sa bisan unsang pinulongan, ug kini walay bayad.**

To find out more about Open Bible Stories, visit [[https://unfoldingword.org/stories/|openbiblestories.com]].

==== How You Can Help ====

Feel free to join the Open Bible Stories translation team and start a new translation, complete one that has already been started, or help check a completed translation. Learn more about [[:en:get-started|how you can get started]] or read the [[:en:obs:help|help section]].
