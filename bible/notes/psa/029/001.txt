===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:ceb:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **A psalm of David**  - This can also be written as a sentence: “This is a psalm of David” or “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
  * **sons of the mighty**  - AT: "mighty men" (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **that his name deserves**  - The phrase "his name" refers to Yahweh or his reputation. AT: "as he deserves" (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]}
  * **that is suitable**  - "that is right"
