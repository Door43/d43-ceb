===== Translation Notes: =====

Moses is still talking to the people of Israel.

  * **to know** - "to reveal" or "to show"
  * **what was in your heart** - The heart is a symbol of a person's character. AT: "what kind of people you are" or "how you would behave." (See: [[ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
