====== 2 Chronicles 08:7-8 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 7 Solomon forced people from many other groups who were not Israelites to work for him like slaves. They were people from the Heth, Amor, Periz, Hiv, and Jebus people-groups.
\v 8 They were descendants of groups whom the Israelites had not completely destroyed. Solomon forced them to become his slaves, and they are still slaves.
</usfm>

===== UDB: =====

<usfm>
\v 7 Solomon forced people from many other groups who were not Israelites to work for him as if they were slaves. They were people from the Heth, Amor, Periz, Hiv, and Jebus people groups.
\v 8 They were descendants of groups whom the Israelites had not completely destroyed. Solomon forced them to become his laborers, and they are still that at this present time.
</usfm>
