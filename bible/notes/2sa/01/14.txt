===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **"Why were you not afraid to kill Yahweh’s anointed king with your own hand?"** - David uses this question to rebuke the Amalekite. ([[:ceb:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Yahweh’s anointed king** - This phrase refers to Saul. (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **your own hand** - This phrase means the Amalekite did something. (See: [[ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **struck him down, **  - This phrase means killed him. - (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **your blood is on your head**  - This phrase refers to the young man's own testimony that he killed the king of Israel,so he has now brought about his own imminent death. (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
