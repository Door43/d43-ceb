===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **walked in the ways of Asa, his father** - AT: "did the same things that Asa, his father, had done" (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **what was right in the eyes of Yahweh** - AT: "what Yahweh considered to be right" (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:ceb:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **the shrines were still not taken away** - AT: "he still did not take away the shrines" (See: [[:ceb:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
