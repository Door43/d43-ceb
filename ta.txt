====== Nia naka sa translation Academy ======

Ang "translatonAcademy" kay nagtumong nga ang bisan kinsa, ug bisan asa makatuon kung unsaon paghubad sa Biblia ngadto sa ilang sariling pinulongan, o aron makatabang sa usa ka tao nga gusto mobuhat sa mao nga butang. Kini gituyo nga masabtan sa tanan, nag-una o sa insaktong panahon (o ang duha, kung gikinahanglan), modular, ug larawan-on, anaa sa web ug offline nga mobile video formats.

Edit

===== Overview of translationAcademy =====

translationAcademy has been created with four manuals to help answer questions pertaining to translation, checking, publishing, and technical problems.

  *

[[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/translate/toc_transvol1_2|Translation Manual]] - Teaches translation theory and answers many practical translation questions.

  *

[[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/checking/toc_checkvol1_2|Checking Manual]] - Teaches checking theory and answers many practical checking questions.

  *

[[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/tech/toc_techvol1_2|Technology Manual]] - Contains tutorials on how to use the various technology tools that we have created.

  *

[[https://door43.org/en/ta/vol1/process/toc_processvol1_2|Process Manual]] - Provides a step-by-step guide through the setup, translation, checking, publishing, and distribution phases.
