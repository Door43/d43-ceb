====== Jacob, Israel ======

===== Ipasabot: =====

Jacob was the second of the twin sons that were born to Isaac and Rebekah. The first twin born was Esau.Si Jacob ang ikaduhang anak nga lalaki nga natawo sa anak nga kaluha ni Isaac ug Rebeka. ang unang natawo nga kaluha mao si Esau.

  * Ang ngalan nga Jacob pasabot  "malimbongon o mangingilad."
  * Si Jacob usa ka tawong utokan ug malinglahon. Makakita siya'g mga pamaagi sa pagkuha  sa panulondong katungod sa duha ka panganay ug sa panalangin gikan sa iyang magulang nga lalaki, nga si Esau.
  * Nasuko si Esau ug nagplano sa pagpatay kaniya, unya nibiya si Jacob sa iyang yutang natawhan. Pero wala madugay nibalik siya ug nagpuyo nga malinawon uban sa iyang igsoong lalaki.
  * Ang pangalang Jacob giilisdan sa Dios ug pangalang Israel, nga nagpasabot nakigbisog uban sa Dios."
  * Ang Dios nagbantay sa iyang pakigsaad kang Abraham ug sa iyang mga kakaliwatan ngadto sa anak nga lalaki ni Abraham nga si Isaac, ug sa anak nga lalaki ni Isaac nga si Jacob.
  * Jacob had twelve sons. Their descendants became the twelve tribes of Israel.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:07-01|[07-01]]]** As the boys grew up, Rebekah loved **Jacob**, but Isaac loved Esau. **Jacob** loved to stay at home, but Esau loved to hunt.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:07-07|[07-07]]]**  **Jacob** lived there for many years, and during that time he married and had twelve sons and a daughter. God made him very wealthy.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:07-08|[07-08]]]** After twenty years away from his home in Canaan, **Jacob** returned there with his family, his servants, and all his herds of animals.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:07-10|[07-10]]]** The covenant promises God had promised to Abraham and then to Isaac now passed on to **Jacob**.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:08-01|[08-01]]]** Many years later, when **Jacob** was an old man, he sent his favorite son, Joseph, to check on his brothers who were taking care of the herds.
