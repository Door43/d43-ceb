====== Lazarus ======


===== Kamatuoran: =====

Si Lazarus higala ni Jesus nga iyang gibanhaw gikan sa kamatayon.

  * Si Lazarus adunay duha ka igsoon nga babaye nga ginganlan ug Mary ug Martha, sila nagtuo usab kang Jesus.
  * Pagkahuman ug banhaw ni Jesus kang Lazarus gikan sa kamatayon, ang mga Judio nga pangulo nasuko ug nangitag pamaagi nga mapatay nila silang duha.

(Posibleng Paagi sa Paghubad: [[:ceb:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-01|[37-01]]]** One day, Jesus received a message that **Lazarus**  was very sick. **Lazarus**  and his two sisters, Mary and Martha, were close friends of Jesus.
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-02|[37-02]]]** Jesus said, "Our friend **Lazarus**  has fallen asleep, and I must wake him."
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-03|[37-03]]]** Jesus' disciples replied, "Master, if **Lazarus**  is sleeping, then he will get better." Then Jesus told them plainly, "**Lazarus**  is dead."
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-04|[37-04]]]** When Jesus arrived at **Lazarus'**  hometown, **Lazarus**  had already been dead for four days.
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-06|[37-06]]]** Jesus asked them, "Where have you put **Lazarus**?"
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-09|[37-09]]]** Then Jesus shouted, "**Lazarus**, come out!"
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-10|[37-10]]]** So **Lazarus**  came out! He was still wrapped in grave clothes.
  * **[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:37-11|[37-11]]]** But the religious leaders of the Jews were jealous, so they gathered together to plan how they could kill Jesus and **Lazarus**.
