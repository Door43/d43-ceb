====== Elias ======

===== Ipasabot: =====

Si Elias ang usa sa pinakaimportanting propeta sa Yahweh. Gipropesiya ni Elias sa dihang si Ahab ang hari sa  amihanang gingharian sa Israel.

  * Giatubang ni Elias si Ahab tungod kay giingganyo ni Ahab ang mga tawo sa pagsimba sa mga dios-dios.
  * Naghatag ang Dios ug pagkaon ug pinuy-anan para kang Elias sa milagrosong mga pamaagi.
  * Naghimo ug daghang mga milagroso ang Dios pinaagi kang Elias ug gipakita sa Israel  nga si Yahweh lang ang bugtong ug tinuod nga Dios.
  * Giatubang ni Elias ang 450 ka mga propeta ni Baal ug gipakita nga si Baal dili tinuod nga dios. Isip resulta, ang mga tawo sa gingharian sa Israel gipatay sa mga propeta ni Baal ug temporaryo lang nga nagdayeg kang Yahweh.
  * Si Elias wala namatay gidala siya ngadto sa langit nga buhi. Unya mipakita si Elias ug Moises ug misulti uban kang Jesus gatusan ka mga katuigan ang milabay ug andam na si Jesus nga mamatay sa Jerusalem.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]** **Elijah** was a prophet when Ahab was king over the kingdom of Israel.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-02|[19-02]]]** **Elijah** said to Ahab, "There will be no rain or dew in the kingdom of Israel until I say so."
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-03|[19-03]]]** God told **Elijah** to go to a stream in the wilderness to hide from Ahab who wanted to kill him. Every morning and every evening, birds would bring him bread and meat.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-04|[19-04]]]** But they took care of **Elijah**, and God provided for them so that their flour jar and their bottle of oil never became empty.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told **Elijah** to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with Ahab because he was going to send rain again.
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-07|[19-07]]]** Then **Elijah** said to the prophets of Baal, "Kill a bull and prepare it as a sacrifice, but do not light the fire."
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:19-12|[19-12]]]** Then **Elijah** said, "Do not let any of the prophets of Baal escape!"
  ***[[:ceb:obs:notes:frames:36-03|[36-03]]]** Then Moses and the prophet **Elijah** appeared. These men had lived hundreds of years before this. They talked with Jesus about his death that would soon happen in Jerusalem.
