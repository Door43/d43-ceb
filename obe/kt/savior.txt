====== Savior ======

===== Facts: =====

A savior is a person who saves or rescues others from danger. It can also refer to someone who gives strength to others or provides for them.

  * In the Old Testament, God is referred to as Israel's Savior because he often rescued them from their enemies, gave them strength, and provided them with what they needed to live.
  * In the New Testament, "Savior" is used as a description or title for Jesus Christ because he provides salvation from the power and penalty of sin.
